Throughout my career, I have worked across multiple industries, which strengthened my adaptability, product mindset, and decision-making in high-ambiguity environments. I operate with a hands-on, ownership-driven approach, contributing from architecture definition to production operations while balancing engineering quality, delivery speed, and measurable business impact.

- I work across the full software lifecycle with a strong focus on **architecture, scalability, performance, resilience, and engineering excellence**.
